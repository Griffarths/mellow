### Typical symptoms

Here are the main signs of a tension headache:

- Diffuse, non-throbbing pain
- A sense of pressure or tightness
- Mild to moderate intensity
- No nausea
- Physical activity is usually well tolerated

> Migraine is a chronic neurological disease. It is never just a headache, and its impact on daily life is often underestimated.

### The four phases

1. The prodromal phase, which can occur up to 48h before the attack
2. The aura, present in about 20% of people with migraine
3. The headache phase, lasting from 4 to 72 hours
4. The postdromal phase, sometimes called the "migraine hangover"
